Market Live Mobile App

An iOS & Android mobile application for searching various product & service providers across Ichalkaranji. Being aware of today`s mobile world it is very necessary to keep our city`s information updated to this technology and make it handy to the society. Keeping this in mind we came up with the idea of mobile app which will have all the information regarding our city on one click.
The information from ‘food’ to ‘house’ and from ‘hair cutting’ to ‘banks’ everything is in one mobile app. The information about where to get ‘delicious food’ as well as where to apply for ‘adhaar card’ is made available in the app. More about we can say if anything about Ichalkaranji city comes to your mind please search it on our mobile app and get updated.
